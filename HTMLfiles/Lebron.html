LeBron James

LeBron Raymone James, known as King James, or THE KING, is one of our competitors for the title. With his career comes multiple achievements and counting. He is greatly compared with Jordan as he has also brought a lot to the table. LeBron entered the league at only 18 years old and had already started to dominate. He led the Cavs' 17-65 (W-L) losing team to a 35-47 (W-L) team during his rookie season, which then turned to 42-40, then 50-32, and eventually 66-16. LeBron now has 4 Championnship rings at a 4-6 (W-L) record in the NBA Finals and is looking to make that a 5-6 record. He has great longevitt considering that he has been in the NBA for more than HALF of his life.


Achievements:

  • 4-time NBA Champion (2012,2013,2016,2020)
  • 2004 Rookie of The Year and All-Rookie Team
  • 4-time MVP
  • 4-time Finals MVP
  • 18-time All-NBA Team
  • 3-time Best Male Athlete ESPY Award (2012,2013,2016)
  • 5-time All Defensive Team
  • Most All-Around Player in NBA
  • 10-time NBA Finalist
