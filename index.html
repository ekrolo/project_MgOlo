NBA: Who is The G.O.A.T?

The G.O.A.T or The Greatest Of All Time, is the title given to the greatest to ever do it, usually in a sport. In this case, who is the "Greatest Of All Time" in basketball, and the NBA or the National Basketball Association? Countless arguments and broken friendships have been caused by this topic. There will never be a clear answer to this question as it's impossible that everyone will agree on a single player taking the title. However, this site will help you decide on who YOUR G.O.A.T is. All about the player, history, achievements, iconic moments, of the Top 3 competitors for this title, as well as other notable players, will be found in here.
